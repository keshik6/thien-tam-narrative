Natural Aromatic Facial Mask


Here at Thien Tam, our products revolve around natural herbs, such as Bac Ha, Ngai Loi, Hoac Huong and Ngu Tram that when mixed together emit a revitalising scent. These uniquely handcrafted face masks contain packets of herbs to provide an energising feel to the incoming air while filtering out smoke and dust particles. The scent from the herbs also calm the nerves, clear the respiratory tract and are naturally anti-bacterial. Not only does the herbal formula improve overall health, it is known to be able to treat illnesses without the need for chemical medicine. In a story shared by Ms Thuan, there was a man who travelled all the way from Phan Thiet to Ho Chi Minh city to purchase the herbs in order to alleviate his daughter’s breathing difficulties. Eventually, his daughter got better just by relying on her herb mixture embedded within the face mask. Every mask that is sold contributes 1000 Vietnamese Dong to the charity.

Land is the origin, our delicate ecosystem


The usage of nylon/ plastic bags are certainly harmful to our environment. Vietnam discards more than 30 billion plastic bags per year, as households in Vietnam use four plastic bags per day, on average. Of note, only 17% of plastic bags are reused. This causes many devastating effects on our environment. Plastic pollution in the ocean caused the death of nearly 100 million marine animals each year, due to their accidental consumption of plastic waste.


Meanwhile, plastic pollution on land is apparent in their long biodegradation of 400 years and toxic chemicals produced. Hence we should start using recyclable bags to reduce plastic waste for the environment. We have two types of recyclable bags, netting bag and cloth bag. Both are made of different materials and come in different sizes but they serve the same function; Saving the environment. So get one of these today!
